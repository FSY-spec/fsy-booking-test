1. About Free Spirit Yoga

Free Spirit Yoga ("FSY", "we", "us") is a private yoga space operated by Halla, based in Ar-Ruwais, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. FSY offers in-person and online yoga classes including Yin Yoga, Restorative Yoga, Yoga Nidra, Sound, and related wellness sessions.

These Terms & Conditions govern your use of the FSY booking app and all related services. By accessing the app or booking a class, you agree to be bound by these terms.

2. Eligibility

  • You must be 18 years of age or older to create an account and book classes independently.
  • Minors may participate only with explicit consent from a parent or legal guardian, who assumes full responsibility.
  • Ladies-only classes are restricted to female students. FSY reserves the right to enforce this restriction at the time of booking.

3. Account Registration

  • You may register using WhatsApp, Google, or Apple login.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account.
  • You must provide accurate information including your name, contact details, and any relevant health conditions.
  • FSY reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms.

4. Bookings

  • All bookings are subject to availability and must be confirmed through the app.
  • Full payment is required at the time of booking.
  • A booking confirmation will be sent to your preferred communication method (WhatsApp or email).
  • FSY reserves the right to cancel or reschedule classes. In such cases, a full refund or class credit will be issued.

5. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • 8 or more hours before class: You may cancel freely. The class will be returned to your package or refunded as credit.
  • 4 to 8 hours before class: You may cancel. A live countdown is shown in the app. The class will be returned to your package or refunded as credit.
  • Less than 4 hours before class: Cancellation is not permitted. The class counts as used and is non-refundable.
  • Late registration (within 4 hours of class): The late drop-in rate (SR 120) applies automatically. No cancellation is possible for late registrations.
  • All payments are non-refundable except where a class is cancelled by the instructor, in which case a full refund is issued.
  • Classes are transferable — a friend or family member may attend in your place.

6. Class Packages

  • Packages are valid for 3 months from the date of the first class attended (not the purchase date).
  • Expired packages may be reactivated during a 3-month grace period at SR 10 per class.
  • Packages are non-refundable once purchased.
  • A second package purchased while one is active will remain dormant and activate automatically once the current package is complete.
  • Each package allows one freeze of 1 to 4 weeks. The package validity is paused and resumes when the freeze ends or when you next attend a class, whichever comes first.

7. Drop-In Classes

  • Early Bird Drop-In (SR 100) is available when booking more than 4 hours before class.
  • Late Drop-In (SR 120) applies when booking within 4 hours of class.
  • Drop-in payments are non-refundable.

8. Gift Cards & Gifted Classes

  • Gift cards have no expiry date and can be used toward any class or package.
  • Gift cards are non-refundable once purchased.
  • Gifted classes are deducted from the gifter's package immediately upon sending and are non-reversible.
  • If a gifted class is sent to a number not registered in the system, it will be logged and held for the recipient until they create an account.

9. Health & Safety

  • You are responsible for determining whether participation is appropriate for your health condition and should seek medical advice where necessary.
  • You agree to inform the instructor at the start of each class of any injuries, medical conditions, or physical limitations.
  • You agree to practice within your personal limits and may rest or modify any movement at any time.
  • To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, FSY and its instructor are released from liability for injury, loss, or damage arising from participation in any class, workshop, or event, except where caused by gross negligence or wilful misconduct.
  • FSY services are not a substitute for medical care. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, seek immediate medical attention.

10. Photography & Recording

11. Intellectual Property

12. Payments

13. Communication

By creating an account, you consent to receiving booking confirmations, class reminders, and service-related notifications through your preferred communication method (WhatsApp or email). You may update your preferences at any time in your Profile settings.

14. Waitlist

15. Termination

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).

1. Who We Are

Free Spirit Yoga ("FSY", "we", "us") is operated by Halla, based in Ar-Ruwais, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. This Privacy Policy applies to the FSY booking app and all related services.

Contact: thefreespirityoga@gmail.com  |  WhatsApp: +966 56 463 6203

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you create an account or use the app:

  • Identity data: First name, last name, gender, birth year
  • Contact data: WhatsApp number, email address
  • Health data: Injuries or health conditions you choose to share (optional)
  • Booking data: Classes booked, attendance history, class pass status
  • Payment data: Transaction records, payment method type (we do not store full card numbers)
  • Technical data: Login method, device type, session data
  • Communication data: Your preferred communication method (WhatsApp or email)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To send booking confirmations, class reminders, and package updates
  • To enforce class eligibility (e.g. Ladies-only classes)
  • To manage your class package, freeze requests, and credit balance
  • To send gift class notifications to recipients
  • To improve the app and our services
  • To comply with legal obligations under Saudi Arabian law

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL):

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil your booking and account
  • Consent: For optional data such as health notes and marketing communications
  • Legitimate interests: To operate and improve our services
  • Legal obligation: Where required by Saudi law

5. Health Data

Health notes (injuries or medical conditions) are optional and visible only to the FSY instructor. This information is used solely to provide appropriate modifications during class. It is never shared with third parties.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider (data stored securely in the cloud)
  • Tap Payments / Stripe / PayPal: Payment processors, who handle transactions under their own privacy policies
  • Unifonic: WhatsApp messaging provider, used only to deliver booking notifications
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider

All third-party providers are bound by data processing agreements and are required to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Booking and class history is retained for a minimum of 5 years for record-keeping purposes.
  • If you delete your account, all personal data associated with your account will be deleted, except for your name and class attendance history, which may be retained for operational and record-keeping purposes.
  • Payment records are retained as required by Saudi financial regulations.

8. Your Rights (PDPL)

Under Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • Authentication is handled securely via Supabase with OTP verification.
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by certified payment providers. FSY does not store card numbers.
  • Access to student data in the admin panel is restricted to the FSY instructor only.

10. Cookies & Local Storage

The FSY app uses local storage to maintain your login session. No third-party tracking cookies are used. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

11. Children's Privacy

The FSY app is not intended for children under 18. If a minor participates in classes, a parent or legal guardian must register on their behalf and accepts full responsibility for the account.

12. Photography & Recording

Photographs or recordings of identifiable students will only be taken and used for promotional purposes with the student's prior consent.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. You will be notified of significant changes via WhatsApp or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and any applicable regulations issued by the Saudi Data & AI Authority (SDAIA).
