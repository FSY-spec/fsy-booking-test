1. About Free Spirit Yoga

Free Spirit Yoga ("FSY") is a private yoga space operated by Halla, based in Ar-Ruwais, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. By accessing the app or booking a class, you agree to be bound by these terms.

2. Eligibility

  • You must be 18 or older to create an account independently.
  • Minors may participate only with parent or guardian consent.
  • Ladies-only classes are restricted to female students.

3. Bookings

  • All bookings are subject to availability and must be confirmed through the app.
  • Full payment is required at the time of booking.
  • A booking confirmation will be sent to your preferred communication method.

4. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • 8+ hours before class: Cancel freely — class returned to your package.
  • 4–8 hours before class: Cancel with live countdown — class returned to package.
  • Less than 4 hours: No cancellation — class counts as used.
  • Late registration (within 4 hours): Late drop-in rate (SR 120) applies automatically.
  • Classes are transferable to a friend or family member.

5. Class Packages

  • Valid for 3 months from the date of first class attended.
  • Expired packages may be reactivated within 3 months at SR 10 per class.
  • Non-refundable once purchased.
  • One freeze allowed per package (1–4 weeks).

6. Drop-In Classes

  • Early Bird (SR 100): booking more than 4 hours before class.
  • Late Drop-In (SR 120): booking within 4 hours of class.
  • Non-refundable.

7. Gift Cards & Gifted Classes

  • Gift cards have no expiry and are non-refundable.
  • Gifted classes are deducted immediately and non-reversible.

8. Health & Safety

  • You are responsible for determining whether participation is appropriate for your health.
  • Inform the instructor of any injuries or limitations before class.
  • To the fullest extent permitted by law, FSY is released from liability for injury arising from participation, except where caused by gross negligence or wilful misconduct.

9. Photography & Recording

Photographs or recordings of identifiable students will only be used for promotional purposes with prior consent.

10. Payments

  • Payments processed via Tap Payments (Mada, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay).
  • FSY does not store your card details.
  • VAT does not apply to FSY classes.

11. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Disputes are subject to the courts of Jeddah.
